Personality Tests

Myers-Briggs Test
After taking the Myers-Briggs personality test, the personality type I was given was INFP. This personality type is one that is introverted and appears calm however are very driven by emotion. This comes as no surprise to me as I do not generally like interacting with new people or expressing my thoughts and ideas if I do not have to. The INFP personality type tends to be introverted meaning that in a team situation, someone with this personality type will definately be required to step out of their comfort zone as in a team situation they will be required to work with others and input ideas.
